Welcome to RunG! If you encounter any issues while using the app, please refer to the FAQ below or contact us via email.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can't log in or register: Please check your internet connection and restart the app. If the issue persists, contact us directly.
  • Inaccurate GPS tracking: Ensure that location permission is granted and try again in open outdoor areas.
  • Data not syncing: Make sure you're logged in and connected to the internet.
  • UI not updating: Try reinstalling the app to get the latest version.
