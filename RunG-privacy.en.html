RunG Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 18, 2025

Thank you for using the RunG app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information (Apple ID or email)
  • Running data (route, pace, duration, distance, cadence, etc.)
  • Device information (OS version, device model, language settings)
  • Weather data (for display purposes, based on your location)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To display and analyze your running sessions
  • To provide local weather updates based on your location
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Permissions Required

  • Location Access: To record routes and retrieve weather data
  • Network Access: To sync data (if initiated by you) and fetch weather info

4. Data Security

We use local storage and Supabase cloud services to protect your data. Your information will never be accessed or shared without your explicit consent.

5. Your Rights

  • You can view, edit, or delete your running data at any time
  • You can reset your account or clear app data via the settings
  • For full account deletion, please contact us
